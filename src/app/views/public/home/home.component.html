What is RRR?

RRR is a software platform which enables to share/donate items with the unprivileged or needy people.

Using this platform people can connect with each other who shares the common goal.

It enables people to give back to the community. They can donate Food, Clothes, Books, Furniture, etc.

Why RRR?

RRR is a vision to serve the community.

It started when we realize that 1 PG in Bangalore throws away 15 - 20 persons food every night when a lot of unprivileged people sleeping with empty stomach

In the 2022 Global Hunger Index, India ranks 107th out of the 121 countries with sufficient data to calculate 2022 GHI scores. With a score of 29.1, India has a level of hunger that is serious.

One of the most important things you can do on this earth is to let people know they are not alone.
What are you waiting for. Join us to work on this noble cause.
